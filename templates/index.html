FAQ

Clear, honest answers about verification.

Is my gift card information safe?

Yes. All gift card codes are processed using encrypted connections, and we do not permanently store your card details. Your privacy and security are our top priority.

How does the validation process work?

When you submit a gift card code, our system securely checks it against supported issuer validation channels and internal verification rules. The process takes only a few seconds and returns the current status of the card.

Which gift cards can be validated on this platform?

We support a wide range of popular gift cards including Amazon, Apple, Google Play, Steam, PlayStation, Netflix, and more. Available card types may vary depending on region and issuer availability.
